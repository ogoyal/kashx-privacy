Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 27, 2025

Information We Collect

KashX is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best expense tracking experience:

  • Local Data: All your expenses, categories, and budgets are stored locally on your device
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to improve the app (can be disabled)
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to fix bugs and improve stability

How We Use Your Information

Your financial data remains private and is used solely for:

  • Providing expense tracking and budgeting features
  • Generating personal reports and insights
  • Improving app performance and stability

We never sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Your financial data is protected through multiple layers of security:

  • Local Storage: Data is stored securely on your device using iOS security features
  • No Cloud Storage: Your financial data is not stored on our servers
  • Access Control: Only you have access to your financial information

Third-Party Services

KashX integrates with trusted Apple services to enhance your experience:

  • App Store: For app distribution and updates
  • StoreKit: For subscription management
  • System Features: Notifications, shortcuts, widgets

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Data Access: View all your stored data within the app
  • Data Export: Export your data anytime in multiple formats
  • Data Deletion: Delete your data locally at any time
  • Analytics Control: Enable or disable analytics in Settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date
  • Notify users through the app
  • Provide clear information about changes

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • In-App: Use the "Contact Support" feature in Settings

We're committed to protecting your privacy and will respond promptly to your inquiries.
